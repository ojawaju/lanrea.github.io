Requesting Account Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account through our app.
  2. Navigate to the Account page.
  3. Click on "More..." and then select "Close Account".
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm the deletion.

Once your request is processed, your account will be permanently deleted along with the following data:

  • Personal profile information
  • Uploaded content and data
  • Interaction records and history
  • Communities you created will not be deleted. They will be transferred to another user. If no suitable user is found, the community may then be deleted.

Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes for a period of up to 6 months after account deletion.
