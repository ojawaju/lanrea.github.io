Features
Events
Ojawaju is the ultimate app for Africans in the diaspora to stay connected with cultural events around them. Whether you're looking for sports matches, music concerts, comedy shows, community meetups, dance parties, or charity events, Ojawaju has you covered.
Community
Ojawaju is your go-to app for joining vibrant African communities abroad. Whether you're looking for a football group, a church community, cultural associations, or hobby groups, Ojawaju helps you find and connect with people who share your values.
Services
Ojawaju makes it easy for Africans in the diaspora to find and support services around them. From African restaurants and salons to financial consultants and legal services, Ojawaju connects you to trusted, culturally aligned providers in your area.
Friendship
Ojawaju is the perfect app for Africans in the diaspora to connect with like-minded individuals and build meaningful friendships. Whether you’re looking to meet new people, share your culture, or bond over shared experiences.

About Us
At Ojawaju, we are passionate about reconnecting Africans in the diaspora with their roots, culture, and community. Our platform is designed to help you feel at home no matter where you are, by connecting you to local African communities, cultural events, and businesses that share your heritage.
Our Mission
To bridge the gap between Africans living abroad and their rich cultural heritage.
